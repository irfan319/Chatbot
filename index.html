Prevention & Precautions

Protect your skin and hair with these essential preventive measures

Skin Disease Prevention

  • Use sunscreen with SPF 30+ daily
  • Cleanse skin gently twice daily
  • Moisturize regularly to maintain skin barrier
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure (10am-4pm)
  • Stay hydrated - drink 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in antioxidants
  • Avoid touching face with unclean hands
  • Change pillowcases regularly

Hair Disease Prevention

  • Use mild, sulfate-free shampoos
  • Avoid excessive heat styling tools
  • Protect hair from chlorine and salt water
  • Massage scalp regularly to improve circulation
  • Trim split ends every 6-8 weeks
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on roots
  • Use wide-tooth comb on wet hair
  • Manage stress through meditation/yoga

Natural Remedies

Traditional solutions for skin and hair wellness

Skin Natural Remedies

Aloe Vera Gel

Soothes inflammation, moisturizes, and accelerates wound healing. Apply fresh gel directly to skin.

Honey & Turmeric Mask

Antibacterial and anti-inflammatory. Mix 1 tsp turmeric with 2 tbsp honey, apply for 15 minutes.

Oatmeal Bath

Relieves itchy, irritated skin. Grind oatmeal, add to lukewarm bath water, soak for 15-20 minutes.

Coconut Oil

Natural moisturizer with antimicrobial properties. Apply thin layer to dry skin areas.

Hair Natural Remedies

Aloe Vera Scalp Mask

Reduces dandruff and soothes scalp. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to scalp, leave for 30 minutes before washing.

Fenugreek Seeds

Strengthens hair and promotes growth. Soak 2 tbsp seeds overnight, blend into paste, apply to hair for 30 minutes.

Apple Cider Vinegar Rinse

Balances scalp pH and adds shine. Mix 2 tbsp with 1 cup water, use as final rinse after shampooing.

Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Rich in vitamin C, prevents premature graying and strengthens hair. Use amla oil or powder in hair masks.
